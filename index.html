How It Works

From inquiry to delivery — here's what to expect when you order with J.Kloting.

01

Send Inquiry

Tell us your garment type, quantity, design idea, and deadline via our contact form, Messenger, or phone.

02

Get Quote & Mockup

We send you a detailed quote and a digital mockup of your design within 24 hours.

03

Approve & Pay

Approve the mockup and settle the 50% downpayment to begin production.

04

Production & Delivery

We print, pack, and ship your order. Final payment upon delivery or pickup.

Turnaround Times

  • DTF (Direct to Film): 3–5 business days
  • Sublimation: 5–7 business days
  • Silk Screen (bulk): 7–10 business days
  • Barong / Custom Apparel: 7–14 business days

Rush orders available — contact us for availability.

Payment & Delivery

  • Payment: GCash · Bank Transfer · Cash on Pickup
  • Terms: 50% downpayment, balance upon release
  • Pickup: Meycauayan City, Bulacan
  • Delivery: Nationwide via J&T Express or Lalamove

Shipping fee shouldered by the customer.
