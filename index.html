Our Story

J.Kloting began in March 2022 as a simple "what if" — a dream two individuals pursued in hopes of building a better future and reaching their aspirations. It started with handmade crochet, grew into retailing shorts, then oversized shirts, and finally became the full custom-printing studio we are today.

Every step taught us the same lesson: quality is everything. We bring that hard-earned craftsmanship to every garment — DTF, sublimation, silk screen, and our signature modern barong — using durable materials and finishing built to last.

Based in Meycauayan, Bulacan, we treat every order, from a single piece to a bulk corporate run, with the same care and dependable turnaround. From that first "what if," J.Kloting became a brand our customers trust.
