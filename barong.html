Care & Fabric

Proper care keeps your barong looking crisp and lasting longer.

Fabric

Our polo barongs are crafted from a premium cotton-polyester blend — lightweight, breathable, and wrinkle-resistant for all-day comfort. The fabric has a clean, semi-formal drape that holds its shape whether you're at a wedding reception or the office.

  • Breathable and lightweight
  • Semi-formal clean drape
  • Wrinkle-resistant
  • Comfortable for all-day wear

Washing & Care

  • Machine wash cold
    Use gentle cycle to preserve fabric and embroidery
  • Hang dry in shade
    Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fabric discoloration
  • Iron on low–medium heat
    Iron inside-out to protect the outer surface
  • Do not bleach
    Bleach will damage the fabric and color
  • Store on a hanger
    Keeps the collar and shoulders in shape
